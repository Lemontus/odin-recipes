Goulash

Description

This easy goulash recipe is quick, easy, and wonderfully satisfying.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Beef
  • Tomato
  • Sauce
  • Macaroni

Steps

  1. Cook the onion in oil, then add the ground been and cook until brown. Add Garlic
  2. Stir in the water, tomatoes and seasonings. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Sir in uncooked noodles, cover, and simmer until pasta is tender.
